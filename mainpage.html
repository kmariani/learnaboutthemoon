The Phases of the Moon!

(scroll over the moon to change the phase!)

Every once in a while you may have looked up at the night sky and noticed that the moon has changed--or completely disappeared! Your eyes aren't playing tricks on you, the moon is changing! Or, what we're seeing is the light bouncing off the moon from the sun as it orbits the earth. You see, the moon doesn't produce its own light but gets it from the sun. The part of the moon we see at night that is glowing is the side of the moon illumated by the sun; the other side is completely dark. Each night as the moon moves across its orbit, the light shifts and we see less and less of the moon until it's compeletly gone!
